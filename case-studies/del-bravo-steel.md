# Case Study: Del Bravo Steel Group

## When Your Shop Runs Smoother Than Your Competitors Think Is Possible

---

## Who They Are

**Del Bravo Steel Group** ‚Äî El Paso, Texas

Full-service structural steel fabricator with their own erection crews. 50+ people between the shop and the field. They do everything from detailing to delivery to setting iron.

Not a huge corporate outfit. A real shop, built by people who know what it takes to get steel out the door and in the air.

---

## The Problem

Del Bravo was growing. More jobs, more pieces, more guys asking questions.

And like most shops, they were drowning in the same problems everyone has:

### The Whiteboard Was the "System"

Job status? Check the whiteboard. If someone remembered to update it. If you could read the handwriting. If you were actually in the shop to see it.

### Group Texts Were Out of Control

Every question went to the group chat:
- *"Did load 4 ship?"*
- *"Where's the W12x26 for job 2847?"*
- *"What's the heat number on that plate?"*
- *"Who's got the keys to the forklift?"*

The same 10 questions, asked 10 different ways, every single day.

### Supervisors Were Secretaries

Shop leads ‚Äî guys who should be running production ‚Äî were spending half their day answering phone calls and texts. The office was constantly interrupting the floor for piece counts, shipping info, and job status.

### Paperwork Was a Nightmare

BOLs done by hand. QC docs scattered across folders. Heat numbers tracked in spreadsheets that nobody updated. Every shipment meant 15 minutes of paperwork that could've been fab time.

> *"We were spending more time chasing information than moving iron."*

---

## What We Built

No off-the-shelf software that tries to make you change how you run your shop.

We built an AI system **around the way Del Bravo actually works**.

### Shop Assistant on Telegram

The guys already had Telegram on their phones. Now they've got an assistant that knows every job, every piece, every load.

Ask it anything:
- *"What's left to fab on 2847?"*
- *"Did the truck leave for Midland?"*
- *"What's the piece mark on grid line 4?"*

Answer in seconds. No phone calls. No walking to the office. No waiting.

### Plugged Into PowerFab

The AI talks directly to their shop system. Real data, real-time. Not some report that's already outdated by the time you print it.

### BOLs That Write Themselves

Load the truck, tell the system what's on it ‚Äî BOL generated automatically with the right piece marks, weights, heat numbers, and descriptions. Done.

### QC On Demand

Need the MTRs for a shipment? Need to pull QC docs for an RFI response? The AI finds it and compiles it. No more digging through file cabinets.
